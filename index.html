aj. cakes

At Aj. Cakes, we believe that baking should be done the old-fashioned way – from scratch. Everything is made lovingly by hand, using only the freshest ingredients. When you order gourmet desserts from us, you can rest assured you’re getting the best, most delicious baked sweet available. Our products are all natural, and made with exceptional ingredients.

Aj. Cakes was founded by Ashley Milley. Her passion for fine baking and recognition of the need for custom cakes helped to mold the idea for the company. Aj. Cakes offers fine pastries, desserts and coffee in a beautiful welcoming cafe.
