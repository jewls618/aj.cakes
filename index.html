aj. cakes

At aj. cakes, we believe that baking should be done the old-fashioned way – from scratch. Everything is made by hand, using only the freshest ingredients. Our products are all natural, and made with exceptional ingredients. We specialize in custom cakes and desserts. When you order gourmet desserts from us, you can rest assured you’re getting the best, most delicious baked sweets available.

aj. cakes was founded by Ashley Milley. Her passion for fine baking and recognition of the need for custom cakes helped to mold the idea for the bakery. aj. cakes offers fine pastries, desserts, custom cake orders and coffee in a beautiful lakeside cafe with free wifi.

Pastries

aj. cakes offers a wide assortment of breakfast breads, pies, tarts, signature desserts, cookies, scones, croissants, cupcakes and treats. Our menu features a selection of customer favorites as well as seasonally-inspired items made with ingredients at their peak of freshness. We prepare our pastries with all-natural ingredients, and also offer a variety of gluten and sugar-free desserts by order.



Pastry case & offerings change over frequently but we offer these cakes & pastries daily:

Scones: blueberry, chocolate chip, vanilla, herb + cheese, almond raspberry

Seasonal Quickbreads: banana, pumkpin, coffee cake

Cake Cakes: rotating flavors in 8" + 10"

Croissants: chocolate, plain, almond

Cupcakes: vanilla, chocolate

Poptars: raspberry
